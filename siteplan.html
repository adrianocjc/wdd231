Marycookins Cookies and Macarons

Site Plan

Site Name

Marycookins Cookies and Macarons — This name highlights the business’s focus on beautifully decorated, handmade cookies and macarons for all occasions. It’s memorable, brandable, and directly communicates the product offering.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an informational hub and showcase for Marycookins Cookies and Macarons. It will allow customers to explore the artistry of the products, view galleries by theme, learn about the business, place orders, read baking tips, and get in touch.

Scenarios

  • How can I order custom cookies or macarons for my event?
  • Where can I see examples of Marycookins’ previous work?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #3CB6A0 (Teal Green) – Used for headers, buttons, and highlights.
  • Secondary Color: #F7B6B2 (Soft Pink) – Used for backgrounds and accent bars.
  • Accent Color: #FFFFFF (White) – Used for text and backgrounds.

These colors are used throughout this document for headings, accents, and backgrounds.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Montserrat – Used in the logo and headings for a modern, friendly look.
  • Secondary Font: Arial, sans-serif – For body text and easy readability.
  • Headings and logo: Montserrat, bold
  • Body text: Arial, regular
